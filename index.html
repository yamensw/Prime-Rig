Why it works

Fast + lightweight

Unlike large AI systems, WebAR doesn’t require a large language model or a data center to “think.” It runs through standard web tech — streamed from a server and rendered on the customer’s device.

Proof of adoption

Used by giants

Major retailers and platforms have normalized AR placement, try-on, and filters — demonstrating demand and usability. Prime Rig packages this into clean, business-ready deliverables.

Business outcomes

More confident buyers

Better product understanding leads to higher conversion, fewer “surprises,” and stronger customer trust — especially when fit, size, and detail matter.

Examples of AR “in the wild” you can reference when pitching: product placement previews, “view in your room,” face/try-on filters, and interactive 3D models embedded in web pages.
